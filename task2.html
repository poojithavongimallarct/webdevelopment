JEE Exam Application

The Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) is a national-level entrance examination for admission to undergraduate engineering programs in India. The exam is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA).

The JEE exam is divided into two parts: JEE Main and JEE Advanced. JEE Main is a qualifying exam, while JEE Advanced is a screening exam for admission to the top engineering colleges in India.
